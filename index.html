BabyCare: A Wearable Agent

Prototyped an AI agent using the Ray-Ban Meta glasses to enable parents of newborns to easily log essential baby activities like diaper changes & sleep schedules, without pulling out the phone from the pocket.

This experiment shows a path towards a world where job-oriented agents could replace apps on the next computing platform.

OS for Agents

A short article summarizing my thoughts about a new relationship model between users, OS and agents on wearables - one that is different from what we have with apps on the current computing platform.

I hope to spark more conversations as a new generation of wearables comes up in the next few years.
